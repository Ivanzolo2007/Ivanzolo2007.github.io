History of Taraz

Taraz is an ancient city with a vibrant history that traces its roots back to the Silk Road era, fostering trade and cultural exchange.

Must-Visit Attractions

Explore Taraz's remarkable landmarks, including the majestic Taraz Castle, the historic Aisha Bibi Mausoleum, and the lively Taraz Zoo.

Taraz Cuisine

Indulge in the exquisite Central Asian flavors of Taraz with traditional dishes such as plov, beshbarmak, and manty.
