History of Taraz

Taraz is one of the oldest cities in Kazakhstan, with a rich history dating back to ancient times. It was a key stop on the Silk Road, serving as a hub for trade and cultural exchange.

Must-Visit Attractions

Taraz offers a plethora of attractions for visitors. Don't miss the stunning Taraz Castle, the ancient Aisha Bibi Mausoleum, and the vibrant Taraz Zoo.

Taraz Cuisine

The local cuisine in Taraz is a delightful blend of Central Asian flavors. Be sure to try traditional dishes like plov, beshbarmak, and manty when you visit.
